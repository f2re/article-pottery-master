### The "Sincere Opening" Protocol
*   **The Whisper**: Don't shout. Start with a quiet observation.
    *   *Bad*: "ATTENTION ALL CERAMIC LOVERS! READ THIS NOW!"
    *   *Good*: "Have you ever held a stone that felt warm, like a living creature?"
*   **The Emotional Gap**: "There is a reason why cracked bowls cost more than perfect ones. It's about forgiveness."
*   **Avoid Generic**: "In this article..." (Instantly kills the vibe).

### Copywriting Formulas (Updated)
*   **PAS (Problem-Agitation-Solution)**: But softer.
    *   *Agitation*: "In a world of plastic perfection..."
    *   *Solution*: "...only the scar on the clay tells the truth."
*   **AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)**: Focus on *Interest* through Sensory Detail.
